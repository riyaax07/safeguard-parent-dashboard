*maifest.json : It works as ID card (introduces the extension to the chrome)
    This is the extension asking Chrome for specific powers. Chrome won't let an extension do anything unless it asks first.

webNavigation → "let me watch when the user goes to a website"
storage → "let me save data locally on this device"
tabs → "let me control browser tabs"
alarms → "let me set a repeating timer"
background tells where exactly is the brain of this extension
actiont tells where to look at when someone clicks the extension
background.js is the main brain 
  it has 4 jobs
  1. load the blocklist from supbase
  2.check if the domain is blocked
  3.watch the navigation
  4.log every visit to supbase
